## Greek Ideals

<div style={{ 
  marginTop: '1.5rem',
  marginBottom: '1.5rem',
  padding: '1.5rem',
  backgroundColor: 'var(--bg-secondary, #f8f9fa)',
  borderRadius: '8px'
}}>

**Greek Ideals** refer to the fundamental principles of beauty, proportion, and harmony that guided classical Greek architecture and art. These ideals emphasized:

- **Proportion**: The mathematical relationships between different parts of a structure
- **Symmetry**: Balanced and harmonious arrangements
- **Clarity**: Clear visual hierarchy and communication
- **Elegance**: Refined aesthetics that stand the test of time

In archUI, we apply these same principles to digital design, ensuring that components are well-proportioned, harmonious, and elegant.

</div>

## Architecture Treatise

<div style={{ 
  marginTop: '1.5rem',
  marginBottom: '1.5rem',
  padding: '1.5rem',
  backgroundColor: 'var(--bg-secondary, #f8f9fa)',
  borderRadius: '8px'
}}>

An **Architecture Treatise** is a formal written work that documents architectural principles, rules, and guidelines. Historically, treatises like Vitruvius's _De architectura_ and later works by Renaissance architects established the foundational rules for classical architecture.

ArchUI draws inspiration from these treatises by establishing clear design principles, component patterns, and styling guidelines that serve as the "blueprint" for building consistent digital interfaces.

</div>

## Orders

<div style={{ 
  marginTop: '1.5rem',
  marginBottom: '1.5rem',
  padding: '1.5rem',
  backgroundColor: 'var(--bg-accent-light, #e0e7ff)',
  borderRadius: '8px',
  borderLeft: '4px solid var(--bg-accent, #3b82f6)'
}}>

**Orders** (also known as architectural orders) are the classical styles of column design in ancient Greek and Roman architecture. Each order has distinct characteristics, proportions, and decorative elements that define its visual identity.

In archUI, we use the concept of orders to represent our three main themes: **Doric**, **Ionic**, and **Corinthian**. Each theme follows specific design principles while maintaining harmony within the overall design system.

</div>

    <ul style={{ marginBlock: "0.75rem" }}>

      <li>Volutes (spiral scrolls) on the capitals</li>

    </ul>
    In archUI, the Ionic theme is the <b>default theme</b> and creates a
    welcoming, visually engaging user experience. It features rounded corners,
    generous padding, borderless design, and soft shadows—ideal for calm,
    innovative, warm & inviting, playful, and beauty brands.
